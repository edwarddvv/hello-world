**git fetch** is used to get remote changes on to your computer, but it leaves your local copy unchanged.  In that way, you can view the remote changes and decide whether you want to incorporate them into your code.  If you do, you run **git merge**.  Then your local copy will be the same as the remote one.  **git pull** does these two steps in one go i.e. it collects the changes from the remote repository and applies them to your local copy.
